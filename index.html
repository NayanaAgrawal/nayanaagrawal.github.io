SPOTLIGHT PROJECT: FOLK TALES OF THE WORLD

JIS BOOK PUBLISHING CLUB

President-Founder • Jakarta Intercultural School, Indonesia • 2017-18

I started this project as a way to create fun educational content for underprivileged schools and libraries in my local community in Jakarta. I reached out to other students who I knew were dedicated, passionate, and creative, set the style guide and deadlines, and assembled and formatted our raw content into a full book.

I pitched the project to the school's PTA and obtained a larger grant than I had requested, allowing me to print hardcover copies of the book with high quality binding.

At the end of the school year, we were able to drive by and distribute 60 copies of the book to our community.
